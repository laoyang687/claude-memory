## Search Strategies

### Google Search Operators

```
# Recent AI applications
"AI application" OR "AI implementation" after:2024-01-01

# Specific industry
"healthcare AI" OR "medical AI" use case after:2024-01-01

# Exclude research papers
AI application -arxiv -paper -research

# Specific region
AI application site:.cn (for Chinese sites)
```

## Quality Assessment

When evaluating sources:

✅ **Reliable:**
- Established publications with editorial standards
- Company official blogs (for their products)
- Industry-specific reputable sites

⚠️ **Use with Caution:**
- Personal blogs (verify claims)
- Press releases (may be biased)
- Social media (confirm with primary sources)

❌ **Avoid:**
- Content farms / SEO spam sites
- Sites with excessive ads
- Unverified rumors

## Usage Tips

1. **Start Broad**: Use general tech news sites for daily scanning
2. **Go Deep**: Use AI-specific sources for detailed coverage
3. **Cross-Reference**: Verify important news from multiple sources
4. **Stay Bilingual**: Check both English and Chinese sources for complete picture
5. **Mix Old and New**: Combine established media with emerging platforms
